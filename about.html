Our unique approach

Onshore

Our on site software consultants primary responsibility is to deliver any front-end software requirements, and to provide support to the project stakeholders. The consultant may also be responsible for liaising with the business to ensure requirements are ready for development In lieu of a delivery manager.

Nearshore

Our remote software consultants primary responsibility is to work as a systems architect and support the on premise consultants by building out APIs, managing data and production releases.

Onshore + Nearshore

We are flexible and will work with you to construct the team that best fulfils your requirements.
